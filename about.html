What we do

Dropavo is a B2B platform that sits between product suppliers and independent dropshipping merchants. We curate products from trusted suppliers, provide tools for markup configuration, and automate the order forwarding process.

When a customer places an order on your store, we automatically route it to the appropriate supplier for fulfillment. This means you can focus on marketing and growing your brand while the logistics happen behind the scenes.

Third-party fulfillment

Important: We sell products through our platform, but fulfillment is handled by independent third-party suppliers. This is the standard dropshipping model used by millions of merchants worldwide.

Our primary supplier partner is Zendrop, known for their US-based warehousing, fast shipping times, and quality control processes. We've chosen to integrate with Zendrop because of their merchant-friendly API, transparent tracking, and commitment to quality.

Transparency Notice

We believe in full transparency with our merchants. While we curate and manage products through our platform, actual product storage, packaging, and shipping are performed by our supplier partners. Shipping times, product quality, and fulfillment accuracy are ultimately dependent on these third-party providers. We work closely with our suppliers to maintain high standards, but cannot guarantee every aspect of the fulfillment process.

Why we chose Zendrop

After evaluating multiple supplier networks, we selected Zendrop as our primary integration partner for several key reasons.

Modern API

Clean, well-documented API that enables seamless integration and real-time order synchronization. Built for developers, by developers.

US Fulfillment

Products ship from US warehouses, dramatically reducing delivery times for North American customers compared to overseas fulfillment.

Scalability

Infrastructure designed for growing businesses. Whether you're processing 10 or 10,000 orders daily, Zendrop scales with you.

Quality Control

Rigorous product vetting and quality assurance processes. Products are inspected before shipping to minimize returns and complaints.
